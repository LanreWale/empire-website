About Us

Understanding our role as an intermediary platform.

Empire Affiliate Marketing Hub is a digital platform that connects users with third-party affiliate marketing campaigns. The platform provides access to campaign opportunities, tracking tools, and performance reporting within a centralized interface.

Our role is strictly that of an intermediary. We do not own, control, or operate the affiliate campaigns available on the platform. All campaigns are provided and managed by independent third-party affiliate networks and advertisers.

Users may participate in campaigns by engaging with offers and sharing campaign links using provided tracking mechanisms. Campaign activity and performance data are made available through the platform for visibility and monitoring purposes.

Empire Affiliate Marketing Hub does not provide financial services, investment products, or custodial wallet services. We do not process payouts, hold user funds, or guarantee earnings or outcomes from campaign participation.

All campaign terms, conditions, and compensation structures are determined solely by the respective third-party providers. Users are responsible for reviewing and complying with the terms of each campaign before participation.
