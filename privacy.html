Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

1. Introduction

Empire Affiliate Marketing Hub ("the Platform") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your data when you access or use the Platform.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and any information you provide when contacting us or registering.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the Platform, including pages visited, clicks, and session activity.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system.

3. How We Use Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain access to the Platform
  • To enable participation in affiliate marketing campaigns
  • To track campaign activity and generate performance reports
  • To improve platform functionality and user experience
  • To respond to inquiries and support requests

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

The Platform operates as an intermediary connecting users with third-party affiliate marketing campaigns. As part of this function:

  • Certain data related to campaign interactions (such as tracking identifiers, click activity, and referral data) may be shared with third-party affiliate networks and advertisers for campaign tracking and validation purposes.
  • We do not control how third-party providers use data once it is shared with them. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of those providers where applicable.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal and usage data only for as long as necessary to provide the Platform's services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Platform may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze usage patterns, and support campaign tracking functionality.

9. Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
